About

Hi! I’m María Núñez, a graphic design student with a big passion for creative storytelling, branding, and playful design. I believe in the power of visuals to express emotions, spark curiosity, and connect people — especially when words fall short.

Originally from Peru and now based in Toronto, my work blends vibrant cultural influences with fresh, modern ideas. I love experimenting with illustration, typography, and packaging, always aiming to design with intention and joy.

Whether it's a poster, an app interface, or a logo, I enjoy creating pieces that feel alive and speak for themselves. I'm still growing as a designer, but I’m not afraid to explore, get messy, and learn along theway. Let’s create something bold and meaningful!
